Chapter 1: Quantum Computing Fundamentals

Quantum computing is a multidisciplinary field comprising aspects of computer science, physics, and mathematics that utilizes quantum mechanics to solve complex problems faster than classical computers. The field of quantum computing includes hardware research and application development. Quantum computers leverage quantum mechanical phenomena, such as superposition and entanglement, to create states that can be used to perform computations.
